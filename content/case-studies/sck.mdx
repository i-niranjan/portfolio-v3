## Overview

SCK needed a storefront refresh that could drive stronger conversion while preserving the visual confidence of the brand. The original experience undersold product quality and introduced unnecessary friction before checkout.

## Problem

Browsing felt visually inconsistent, product pages did too little to build confidence, and the handoff into checkout introduced hesitation at the exact moment users were ready to convert.

### Core issues uncovered

- The storefront lacked a clear narrative between discovery and purchase.
- Product value was obvious in the physical offering but not in the digital presentation.
- Checkout momentum dropped when users hit unclear steps or redundant decisions.

## Approach

The redesign tightened the storefront hierarchy, gave product media more authority, and simplified purchase decision points. Every primary action was reframed around momentum, with stronger emphasis on product trust and fewer points of hesitation.
